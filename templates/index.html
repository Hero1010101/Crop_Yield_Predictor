1. Upload Your Crop Data

Data Upload

Upload a CSV file containing your crop data with the following columns:

  • Crop: Name of the crop
  • Crop_Year: Year of cultivation
  • Season: Growing season
  • State: State/region
  • Area: Cultivated area (hectares)
  • Production: Total production (kg)
  • Annual_Rainfall: Annual rainfall (mm)
  • Fertilizer: Fertilizer used (kg/hectare)
  • Pesticide: Pesticide used (kg/hectare)
  • Yield: Crop yield (kg/hectare)

Technical Details
Algorithms Used
  • XGBoost: Extreme Gradient Boosting - known for high performance and accuracy
  • LightGBM: Light Gradient Boosting Machine - efficient and fast training
Features Considered
  • Crop type and variety
  • Seasonal factors
  • Geographical location
  • Agricultural inputs (fertilizer, pesticide)
  • Environmental conditions (rainfall)
  • Historical yield data
